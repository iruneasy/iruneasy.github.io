Weekly Update #2

This was week 25 of 2020 and week 6 of the 5k Training Plan I am following.
Here are the stats from my watch, since it already flipped over to monday, the 7 day overview left out last monday, the 15th:

Only had time to do 4 runs this week.
The missing Monday was an interval workout, Wednesday was a tempo run and the long run, which I did on Friday this week was for 6.5km / 4 Miles ( which ended up being 6.95km). Since these long runs are supposed to be run at an easy pace, the length itself wasn’t a problem at all. But the first 1-2km I had real problems finding that easy pace, but at around 2k I settled into a comfortable pace which i held (with small variations) to the end without much of a problem. Pretty happe with this weeks progress.

Next weeks workouts include an interval run on a hill. The hills here go all over the place with inclination, so I am just going pick one and will keep going up that one. That workout will probably be hard on me, since the runs I always do are pretty much flat with only a small net elevation change.
